A 5,000 sq ft immersive Midtown Manhattan space turned over to a 30-foot graffiti wall and a 16-foot LED with a writer painting all night.

SHiFT Midtown is a 5,000 sq ft immersive event space in the middle of Manhattan — built around 360° projection mapping, 7.1 surround sound, and concert-grade lighting. The venue is built to be reconfigured for every event. For this private party we configured it for graffiti: turned the venue's entire 30-foot main wall into a giant projection digital graffiti canvas running through SHiFT's projector array.

Out in the lobby, a 16-foot LED wall ran in parallel — our writer Seb painted massive pieces on it all night while guests cycled in and out of the main room. Two walls, two scales, one continuous moment. The main-room projection scaled up for impact, the lobby LED scaled down for one-on-one focus.

Few venues let you stretch the platform across this kind of square footage. SHiFT is one of them — and the night was a proof of how far the system scales when the space is built for it.

Eighteen years. Six continents. One idea, done right.

For eighteen years, Graffiti+ has been putting real creative tools in people's hands — in public, at scale, and always with cultural integrity.

Born from the roots of graffiti culture and built for anyone willing to pick up a custom spray can, the platform scales from a 16-foot LED to a 30-foot projection across a single private party. From their home in Vancouver to projects across six continents, Graffiti+ has partnered with luxury venues, brand activations and global brands including Samsung KX, Chanel, Nike, Hennessy and Louis Vuitton to bring authentic, participatory experiences to audiences everywhere.

Two walls. Two scales. One night. The system scales as far as the venue does.
