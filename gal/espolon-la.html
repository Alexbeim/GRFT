Espolòn, LA

Brand Collab

For Espolón Tequila’s 25th anniversary celebration in LA, we built a custom-branded wall that pulled from the brand’s visual language — street art, Mexican heritage, bold colour. A local LA graffiti artist ran the wall all night, walking guests through it and keeping the energy up. Espolón’s aesthetic and graffiti culture speak the same language, so this one came together naturally.
