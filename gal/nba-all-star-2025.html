NBA All-Star Weekend rolled into the Bay Area — and Jayson Tatum stepped up to a Jordan Brand wall that wrote his name back.

NBA All-Star Weekend 2025 took place across San Francisco and Oakland from February 13–16. Jordan Brand's flagship activation — "Unbannable Air" — lived inside Foot Locker's two-story NBA All-Star pop-up at 150 Powell Street in Union Square. We powered the interactive wall. Anthony Edwards, Jayson Tatum and Shai Gilgeous-Alexander rotated through the floor across the four days; Tatum stepped up to the wall and tagged it himself, with the LED carrying his name as he painted.

Fans grabbed custom spray cans and picked Air Jordan stencils and sticker packs from the menu. For the finish, their artwork projected onto a rotating 3D basketball — and then a 3D Jordan sneaker — built specifically for the activation, then took home via AR. The whole loop moved through three formats without breaking stride: wall, object, take-home.
