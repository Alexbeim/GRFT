ComplexCon's first major Asia edition — a thirteen-foot inflatable bunny holding a hundred-inch graffiti canvas, with thirty thousand people moving past it over three days.

ComplexCon — the Complex Networks & NTWRK collectibles, fashion and culture convention that originated in Long Beach — landed in Hong Kong from March 21–23, 2025 at AsiaWorld-Expo, in its Daniel Arsham–presented edition. The show pulled 30,000+ attendees over three days, with the Complex NTWRK retail floor anchored by streetwear drops, exclusive merch and a roster of culture figures.

We partnered with longtime collaborator Carson Ting (Chairman Ting) — fresh off our Lululemon "Create the Future" and Porsche 75th anniversary collaborations — and Hong Kong–based art-toy studio Billion Buns for "Digital Graffiti Space Bun": a 13-foot inflatable Space Bun sculpture by Billion Buns embracing a 100" 4K Graffiti+ digital canvas, flanked by inflatable Graffiti+ and Chairman Ting–branded spray cans on either side. Attendees walked up, picked a Chairman Ting bunny character on the canvas, and painted it live across the three days.

The booth pulled the room. Notable visitors included graffiti legend Futura, sneaker-culture icon jeff staple, designer Henry Chu, and creative Gene Soo — Hong Kong-based TVB covered the activation. For Graffiti+, this was the platform sitting comfortably in front of the people who built the culture it's borrowing from. The bar a writer like Futura applies just by walking past is the bar this booth was built for.

Eighteen years. Six continents. One idea, done right.

For eighteen years, Graffiti+ has been putting real creative tools in people's hands — in public, at scale, and always with cultural integrity.

Born from the roots of graffiti culture and built for anyone willing to pick up a custom spray can, the platform transforms convention floors and culture-forward spaces into shared canvases where artists and audiences paint together in real time. From their home in Vancouver to projects across six continents, Graffiti+ has partnered with Chairman Ting, Billion Buns, Complex NTWRK, Trampt at DesignerCon, Nike, Samsung and Louis Vuitton to bring authentic, participatory experiences to audiences everywhere.

When Futura walks past your installation in Hong Kong, you've earned the booth.
