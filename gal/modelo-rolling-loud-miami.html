Modelo, Rolling Loud Miami

Brand CollabFestival

At Rolling Loud Miami 2024, Modelo ran the LED wall alongside a live mural session from our artist Vic — two things happening at once, both drawing a crowd. Festivalgoers painted Modelo-themed pieces on the wall while Vic’s mural grew behind them. The wall gave people something to do; Vic gave them something to watch. Together they kept the booth moving all weekend.
