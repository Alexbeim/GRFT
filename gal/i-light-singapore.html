Five metres tall.
Zero mess.

Grab a spray can, press the nozzle, and create anything you can imagine on the five-metre-tall canvas. This is graffiti without the paint: no aerosols and no mess — yet the experience still feels entirely tactile.

Over the course of three weeks, thousands will get their chance to create their own unique artwork. Presented by Families for Life as part of the National Family Festival, Infinite Graffiti transforms into a collective canvas where families can create and connect. By removing traditional barriers, we invite people of all generations to step up as artists, turning moments of play into lasting memories.

Eighteen years. Six continents. One idea, done right.

For eighteen years, Graffiti+ has been putting creative digital graffiti tools into people's hands — in public, at scale, and always with cultural integrity.

Born from the roots of graffiti culture and built for anyone willing to pick up a custom spray can, the platform transforms public spaces into shared canvases where individuals and groups paint together in real time. From their home in Vancouver to projects across six continents, Graffiti+ has partnered with world-renowned brands like Louis Vuitton, Nike, Chanel, Hennessy, and Google to bring authentic, interactive experiences to audiences everywhere.

The goal has always been the same: to create a space for real expression and culture that is open to everyone.
