Hyundai — Seoul

Brand Collab

At the 2025 Seoul Mobility Show, we partnered with Hyundai on a multi-layer activation: guests painted on the LED wall, their designs were projected onto rotating 3D crane models, and an AR component let them see the work in space. The whole thing moved through three formats without feeling forced — wall, object, phone. A strong fit for a brand talking about what comes next in how things move.
