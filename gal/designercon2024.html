DesignerCon 2024

Brand Collab

At DesignerCon Las Vegas, we partnered with Trampt to turn 3D models from OG Slick, Superplastics, Nouns, and Marmals into paintable objects on the wall. People customized the toys, scanned a QR code, and took their designs home as AR models to drop anywhere at the event. OG Slick, Woes, Ron the Killer, Goopmasta, and Chris RWK all stepped up — alongside people who'd never touched a can. Both groups figured it out.
