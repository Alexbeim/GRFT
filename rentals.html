LED — Nike Air Jordan 2025

Nike Air Jordan — San Francisco

Graffiti+ teamed up with Nike for an activation that fused sneaker culture with digital art. Guests used Air Jordan-themed tools to create custom graffiti, brought to life with AR on 3D basketball and sneaker models.

LCD — Disney+ Daredevil 2025

Disney+ Daredevil — Toronto Comicon

Graffiti+ partnered with Disney+ for the Daredevil: Born Again activation. Fans created custom Daredevil-themed art with AR features and comic-style stencils, bringing Hell's Kitchen to life in bold, interactive colour.

Projection — Espolòn Tequila

A custom-branded projection wall for Espolón's 25th anniversary. Guests painted in real-time alongside a local graffiti artist, with designs rooted in street art and Mexican heritage.
