Product Customization

Let guests step into your brand's world with interactive on-screen 3D objects. They can rotate, zoom, and paint digital products right on the display, creating a personalized piece of art. Our team custom-builds these 3D experiences to highlight your brand in a playful, memorable, and highly engaging way!

AR Experience

After painting on a 3D object, every creation becomes something users can take beyond the screen. Their design is saved to their phone and instantly viewable in augmented reality — placed into their surroundings at full scale. Whether it's a product, character, or custom model, the AR Experience turns digital artwork into a personal, shareable moment that extends your brand into the real world.

T-Shirts

Bring creations to life on fabric. With our T-Shirt add-on, guests can turn their digital artwork into a custom tee printed live on-site. They design it, we press it, and within minutes they're walking away with wearable art. It's bold, fun, and instantly shareable — the kind of takeaway people actually keep and show off long after the event.

Prints & Stickers

Turn digital creations into physical keepsakes. With our Prints + Stickers add-on, guests can instantly transform their artwork into custom stickers or branded prints — like the 3D train-crate takeaway we produced for a past activation. Whether it's a collectible sticker sheet or a premium card-style print, each piece becomes a tangible reminder of the experience. Designed on screen, held in hand — a perfect blend of digital creativity and real-world merch.

Buttons

Turn artwork into wearable merch. With our Buttons add-on, guests can instantly convert their design into a custom pin-back button — ready to clip onto bags, jackets, or lanyards. It's small, collectible, and super shareable. The perfect way to leave your event with a tiny piece of creativity you can wear anywhere.

Branded Backgrounds

Set the scene before anyone picks up a can. We build fully custom digital environments — branded backdrops, textures, cityscapes, product worlds — that surround the canvas and immerse guests in your brand. Every piece of artwork created lives inside your visual universe, making every photo and share a branded moment without any extra effort.

Custom Spray Can Labels

The spray can is the first thing guests touch — make it yours. We produce custom-labelled cans bearing your brand's visual identity, complete with real aerosol sound effects. They double as a photo prop, a keepsake, and a brand touchpoint before a single mark is made on screen. A small detail that makes a big impression.

Animation & Motion

Bring the wall to life beyond the paint. We can integrate animated elements — characters, particles, brand mascots, falling objects, reactive backgrounds — that respond to or layer over guest artwork in real time. It turns a great experience into a jaw-dropping one, and gives people something they've genuinely never seen before.

Online Gallery

Every piece created at your event lives on. Guests get a personal link to their artwork — shareable to social, downloadable, and hosted in a branded online gallery. Your activation keeps spreading long after the event ends.
