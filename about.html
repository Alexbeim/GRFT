About Graffiti+

Eighteen years. Six continents. One premise.

At Graffiti+, we believe in the power of art as a medium for expression, connection, and transformation. We are passionate about making the world of graffiti accessible to everyone, everywhere, in a way that respects and honors its roots and culture.

We believe in working with brands that get it — those who understand that graffiti is more than just street art; it's a voice for stories untold, a canvas for unbridled creativity, and a symbol of urban identity. That's why we're on the lookout for like-minded collaborators who value this art form as much as we do.

Partnering with us means engaging in a respectful association with graffiti culture. We offer a platform where brands can connect with the community in a meaningful way, ensuring that their involvement isn't just seen but felt and appreciated. Our goal is for the art form and the brand to grow from each other — creating something that feels genuine to your audience, not just visible to them.

We don't just provide a service; we facilitate a cultural exchange that respects and elevates the essence of the culture while adding real value to your brand's identity. With Graffiti+, your brand doesn't just get visibility; it earns cultural relevance and respect.

If that sounds like the kind of partnership you're looking for, get in touch.
