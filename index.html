The Original

We created the digital graffiti wall. In 2008. Everything since is a variation.

Graffiti+ started in Vancouver in 2008 — not as a product, but as a question: what happens when you put real spray-can tools in people's hands, in public, at scale? We started by projecting onto city buildings, asking writers to test it before anyone else got near it. What came back wasn't feedback. It was participation.

Since then we've run 500+ activations on six continents for brands including Louis Vuitton, Nike, Chanel, Hennessy, Porsche, Google, and IBM. The technology has evolved. The premise hasn't. Eighteen years of doing one thing, done right.

FAQ

Common questions.

Does it work outdoors?

Yes. Our LED Wall is built specifically for outdoor use and is fully visible in direct sunlight. The LCD Display and Projection setups work best indoors or in shaded environments. If you're planning an outdoor event, we'll recommend the right configuration for your conditions.

What staffing do you provide?

Every activation includes trained graffiti artist technicians on-site for the full duration of your event. They handle setup, operate the system, guide guests through the experience, and manage takedown. You don't need to source any additional crew.

Can multiple people paint at the same time?

Yes. The LED Wall supports multiple simultaneous painters — the larger the setup, the more users at once. The LCD Display supports 1–2 users and the Projection setup supports 2–3. For high-traffic events, the LED Wall is the right call.

Can we use our own graphics and branding?

Absolutely — and most clients do. We build custom digital stencils, backgrounds, spray can labels, and brand overlays into every activation. Your brand lives inside the experience, not just on a banner beside it.

Can we use an LED screen we already own?

In some cases, yes. Reach out with your screen specs and we can assess compatibility. We've integrated with third-party hardware before and are happy to explore it.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend reaching out at least 4–6 weeks ahead for US events. International activations may require more lead time depending on equipment logistics and crew travel. For large-scale festivals or multi-city tours, earlier is always better.

What kind of events is this right for?

Brand activations, product launches, music festivals, corporate conferences, gallery openings, cultural events, private parties. If you have an audience and want them genuinely engaged, it works. We've deployed in museums, warehouses, parking lots, and Formula 1 paddocks.

What do you need from us on-site?

Standard 110V power and floor space. We bring everything else — equipment, crew, cables, branded cans. Setup typically takes 2–4 hours depending on configuration.
