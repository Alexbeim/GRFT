The Original

Two decades in,
and still perfecting it.
It's our most authentic experience yet—
and we're still pushing.

Graffiti+ started in Vancouver in 2008 — not as a product, but as a question: what happens when you put real spray-can tools in people's hands, in public, at scale? We started by projecting onto city buildings, asking writers to test it before anyone else got near it. What came back wasn't feedback. It was participation.

Since then we've run 500+ activations on six continents for brands including Louis Vuitton, Nike, Chanel, Hennessy, Porsche, Google, and IBM. The technology has evolved. The premise hasn't. Eighteen years of doing one thing, done right.

FAQ

Common questions.

Does it work outdoors?

Yes. The LED configuration is built for outdoor use — modular panels that stay fully visible in direct sunlight. LCD and projection setups work best indoors or in shaded environments. If you're planning an outdoor event activation, we'll recommend the right configuration for your conditions.

What staffing do you provide?

Every activation includes trained graffiti artist technicians on-site for the full duration of your event. They handle setup, operate the system, guide guests through the experience, and manage takedown. You don't need to source any additional crew.

Can multiple people paint at the same time?

Yes. An LED configuration supports multiple simultaneous painters — the larger the setup, the more users at once. LCD supports 1–2 users and projection supports 2–3. For high-traffic events, LED is the right call.

Can we use our own graphics and branding?

Absolutely — and most clients do. We build custom digital stencils, backgrounds, spray can labels, and brand overlays into every activation. Your brand lives inside the interactive graffiti wall experience, not just on a banner beside it.

Do you bring the LED screen, or do we provide it?

Either works. Most setups pair our platform with a screen sourced by your venue or production team — we integrate seamlessly with what's on site. When that's not an option, we can source the right configuration for your space. Send us your specs and we'll figure out the best approach together.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend reaching out at least 4–6 weeks ahead for US events. International activations may require more lead time depending on equipment logistics and crew travel. For large-scale festivals or multi-city tours, earlier is always better.

What kind of events is this right for?

Brand activations, product launches, music festivals, corporate conferences and team events, gallery openings, cultural events, augmented reality events, private parties. If you have an audience and want them genuinely engaged, it works. We've deployed in museums, warehouses, parking lots, and Formula 1 paddocks.

What do you need from us on-site?

Standard 110V power and floor space. We bring the full Graffiti+ rig — controllers, custom cans, software, AV gear, and crew. The display itself we sort with you: a venue-provided LED screen, your own, or one sourced through us. Setup typically takes 2–4 hours depending on configuration.
